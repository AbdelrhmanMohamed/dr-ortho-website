Practical Experience

Dr Abdelkhalek Alzalabany - Consultant of Orthopedic and trauma Surgery

He is interested in Pediatric orthopedic Problems, including Foot, Knee and Hip and upper limb problems.

He is also interested in deformity Correction in Adult and Pediatri in both lower and upper limbs by different Correction methods.

He has special interest in Hip Preservation Surgeries for Adult and Pediatric diseases like Perth's disease, Slipped Capital Femoral Epiphysis, and Avascular Necrosis of the Hip.

He is also interested in Joint Replacement 1ry or Revision total Hip Arthroplasty

He is also interested in Acetabular and Pelvic Fracturs, Complex fractures and trauma of upper and Lower Limbs

He also interested in congenital deficiencies like (Congenital Femoral deficiencies, Tibial Hemimelia, Fibular Hemimelia).

He is also interested in Limb Reconstruction and Lengthening, Non union, Malunion and Pseudoarthrosis in Adult and Pediatric

He has special interest in application of in ilizarov and external fixators in Deformity correction, Limb Reconstruction and Lengthening

He is also interested in Musculoskeletal disorders due to blood disease like Hemophilia, Sickle cell anaemia & Thalassemia.

He is also interested in Cerebral Palsy and Paralytic Condition.

He also interested in management of Hemophilic Pseudotumour and benign bone tumours.
